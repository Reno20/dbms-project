About Our Auction Platform

Who We Are

Welcome to AGM, an auction management platform designed for art collectors, artists, and enthusiasts. Our platform allows users to browse, search, and view art collections, while giving admins the tools to manage artworks and auction events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a simple and secure platform where art lovers can explore and discover various artworks. We aim to make it easy for admins to manage auction schedules and maintain records of artworks without the hassle of complicated systems.

What We Offer

Our platform provides the following features:

  • Browse and search for available artworks based on artist, price, or origin.
  • Track the history of artworks, including previous owners and auction prices.
  • Admins can add new artworks, manage artwork data, and schedule auction events.
  • A secure login and authentication system to ensure data protection.
